about us

At Scrap2New, we're passionate about revolutionizing the way we think about technology and the environment. Our mission is to bridge the gap between innovation and sustainability by refurbishing and repurposing old, discarded electronics into fully functional, like-new devices.
Scrap2New was born out of a desire to make a positive impact on the environment and to provide affordable technology solutions to individuals and organizations. Our team of experts has years of experience in the IT industry, and we've seen firsthand the devastating effects of electronic waste on our planet. We knew that there had to be a better way, and so we set out to create a company that would challenge the status quo and push the boundaries of what's possible.

questions and answers

What is Scrap2New?

Scrap2New is a company that provides sustainable technology solutions, including refurbished electronics, electronic waste recycling, IT asset disposition, data destruction, and sustainable IT consulting services.

What is the goal of Scrap2New's refurbished electronics service?

The goal of our refurbished electronics service is to reduce electronic waste by refurbishing and repurposing old, discarded electronics into fully functional, like-new devices, making affordable technology solutions available to individuals and organizations.

How does Scrap2New's electronic waste recycling service work?

Our electronic waste recycling service collects, dismantles, and recycles old electronics, ensuring that all hazardous materials are handled and disposed of in accordance with international best practices, minimizing the environmental impact of electronic waste.

What is IT Asset Disposition (ITAD), and how can Scrap2New help with it?

IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) is the process of securely and sustainably disposing of retired IT assets. Scrap2New's ITAD services help organizations identify the value of their assets, refurbish or recycle them, and provide a detailed report on the disposition of their equipment.

How does Scrap2New's data destruction service ensure data security?

Our data destruction service uses certified processes to completely erase sensitive data from retired IT assets, ensuring that data is safely and securely destroyed, giving organizations peace of mind that their data is protected
